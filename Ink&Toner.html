To find the ink/toner cartridge number, remove the cartridge from the printer.

Look at the front or side of the cartridge for a combination of numbers and/or letters (ie 678)

To find the printer model, look at the top or front of your printer (ie HP DeskJet 3720).

Selecting the exact SKU number (ie 297X1A) is not necessary.
